Eric Fox's Homepage

I am a postdoctoral statistician with the Western Ecology Division of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. I recently completed a Ph.D. with the UCLA Department of Statistics.

My current research is on the development of statistical models for predicting and mapping the condition of U.S. rivers and streams. The models make use of data from the EPA's National Rivers and Streams Assessment and Stream-Catchment Dataset.

Research Interests

Spatial statistics, point processes, and statistical learning with applications to ecology, seismology, and social networks.
